**Corsie a Colori** ("Colorful Wards") is a non-profit volunteer organisation I co-founded in 2022 together with Claudio Coppola, Santi Fazio, Salvatore La Rocca, Giulia Biondi, Melissa Coco, and Vito Amato. The premise is straightforward: a child's hospital stay is frightening enough without spending it in blank, clinical corridors. Colourful murals — inspired by nature, cartoons, and children's literature — can measurably reduce anxiety and stress for young patients and their families.

We coordinate with medical staff to commission local artists and volunteers to create permanent artworks in wards and waiting rooms. The inaugural intervention took place in August 2023 at the Pediatric First Aid Point of Paternò hospital, with murals inspired by *The Little Prince* by local artist Alessia Santangelo.

## Hospitals reached

Since 2022 we have completed artistic interventions in the following pediatric departments across the Catania province:

1. **Punto di Primo Intervento Pediatrico** — Presidio "SS. Salvatore", Paternò *(inaugural, Aug 2023)*
2. **UOC Clinica Pediatrica** — Presidio "G. Rodolico", Catania
3. **UO Endocrinologia Pediatrica e Neuropsichiatria Infantile** — Presidio "G. Rodolico", Catania
4. **UOC Pediatria** — Presidio "Maria Ss. Addolorata", Biancavilla
5. **UOC Pediatria** — Presidio "Santa Marta e Santa Venera", Acireale *(most recent, Mar 2026)*

The Acireale intervention in March 2026 — featuring superheroes and fantasy worlds by artist Umberto Camelia (Ipnos) — was supported by Leo Club, Lions Club, Rotaract, and Rotary Acireale, and presented at an event attended by ASP Catania leadership.

The project has been endorsed by the **Società Italiana di Pediatria (SIP)** and the **Federazione Italiana Medici Pediatri (FIMP)**.

Corsie a Colori ("Colorful Wards") is a volunteer initiative I co-founded in 2022 to bring art and colour into pediatric hospital wards in Catania. The idea is simple: a children's hospital stay is stressful and often frightening, and even small acts of beauty — a mural, a shared drawing session, a splash of colour on an otherwise clinical wall — can meaningfully reduce anxiety for both patients and their families.

We work directly with pediatric departments, coordinating with medical staff to run creative workshops and install permanent artworks in wards and waiting rooms. To date we have been active in **5 hospitals** across the Catania area.

The project has grown through word of mouth and volunteer energy alone. Every session is run by volunteers — artists, students, professionals — who give their time to make hospital spaces feel a little more human.

If you'd like to get involved or support the initiative, reach out via Instagram.

**Ittinsect** (now rebranding as *Ocean Twist Biotechnology*) is a biotech startup that produces sustainable, high-performance aquaculture feed ingredients through the biotechnological extraction of proteins from insects and agricultural by-products — replacing traditional fishmeal, whose production places enormous pressure on wild fish stocks.

I co-ideated the project in 2020 together with Claudio Boccassini, Sofia Gaudioso, Giovanna Sidaoui, Gennaro Apuzzo, and Alessandro Romano. The team went on to win the **EIT Food Challenge Lab** (1st place, organised by Impact Hub Siracusa with the EU's EIT), and the concept was subsequently developed into a fully incorporated company.

## Impact and milestones

- **EIT Food Challenge Lab** — 1st place (2020)
- **BlueInvest Awards** — finalist, "Sustainable Food and Feed from the Ocean" category (2021)
- **Venice Climathon** — honourable mention
- **Startup3** — 2nd place
- **Techstars Startup Weekend** — 3rd place
- **€750,000 raised** in pre-seed funding (2023), including equity from CDP Venture Capital, Katapult Ocean, Indico Capital Partners, and LVenture Group

The technology targets a fish-in-fish-out (FIFO) ratio below 1, reducing demand on ocean-sourced fish by an estimated 300 billion units annually and cutting aquaculture CO₂ emissions significantly. By end of 2023 the company was delivering 1,500 tonnes of sustainable feed for trout, sturgeon, sea bass, sea bream, salmon, and tilapia.
